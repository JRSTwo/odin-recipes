Lasagna

Description:

Bring me the head of inigo montoya and we'll make a tasty dinner.

Ingredients:

  • Basil
  • Head of Inigo Montoya
  • Bread
  • Catsup
  1. Saw off the skullcap with a bonesaw.
  2. Remove the brain and put it in a big bowl.
  3. Add the whole bottle of Catsup to the bowl.
  4. Mix the contents of the bowl thoroughly. Maybe use a mixer or whip it. Up to personal preference, but Inigo Montoya really got a whipping before he got to you so one might be fitting.
  5. Pour the mixture back into the skull.
  6. Tear up the Basil and sprinkle on top.
  7. Put the cap back on and serve cold. Like his murderers soul.
